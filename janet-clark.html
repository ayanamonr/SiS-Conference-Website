Dr. Janet Clark

Prior to coming to the NIMH, Dr. Clark served as Associate Professor in the Department of Pharmacology and Physiology, Director of the Pharmacology and Physiology Graduate Program and Co-Director of the Drug Discovery and Development Program at Drexel University College of Medicine, Philadelphia, PA. In addition, Dr. Clark has ten years of research experience at a major pharmaceutical company and prior to that did her postdoctoral training at the NIMH. Most recently Dr. Clark has been invited to serve as a member of the Society for Neuroscience Professional Development Committee and she has organized and chaired Career and Professional Development Workshops at the Experimental Biology Annual Meeting for the Pharmacology Education Division of ASPET.

As a senior administrator, Dr. Clark is charged with the development and oversight of the IRP's integrated multidisciplinary training program and with providing support and resources for all training-related activities.
