Dave Israel

Dave Israel is an exploration and space communications projects division architect at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center and serves as principal investigator for the Laser Communications Relay Demonstration (LCRD), which will test foundational laser communications technologies. Israel also led a recent demonstration of DTN technologies.

He received a BS in electrical engineering from Johns Hopkins and an MS in electrical engineering from George Washington University.
