Science in Society Conference

Are you interested in learning more about research, advancements, and internships in the STEM field? Attend the 2018 Howard County Science in Society Conference to connect with STEM professionals from NASA, NIH, NSA and more!

Learn about STEM based career paths in small workshops run by various speakers, and interact with organization representatives to find internship opportunities during a reception at the end of the conference.

Event Details

Date: Monday December 3rd, 2018
Time: From 6:00pm to 9:00pm

  • Dinner will be provided
  • Please park in the parking lot next to the tennis courts

Location: River Hill High School 12101 Clarksville Pike, Clarksville, MD 21029
